Sample, Downloadable Templates

Below, we have some sample templates to help you think through planning for highschool. I would recommend filling these out, or at least thinking them through, before a meeting to make that time the most effective. On their own, these forms are not complete. The 4-year plan below is for college-prep, which may not fit your needs. Also, it is a good idea to make course descriptions. For these added needs, it is best to schedule an appointment with Marit.
