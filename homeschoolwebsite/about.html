My Story

Marit Lunstad holds a B.A. in Speech Communications and Psychology from St. Olaf College. She worked at a law firm in Tokyo and a financial firm in Minneapolis before leaving the business world to raise her four kids.

She has been a home educator for 17 years. For the past 11 years she's been teaching at a homeschool co-op that she founded with a friend. She's passionate about helping students prepare for their career and calling.
