Our Team

Sorcha is the founder of this coffee-shop and you will still see her behind the counter as a barista! Her passion for coffee is almost too intense.

Andrew is one of our fantastic baristas and also manages inventory for the store. He travels to the coffee bean farms to ensure fair trade and the highest quality beans.

Sabrina performs opera in the cafe every evening to create the ideal atmosphere for a relaxed night with friends or a significant other. Her piercing arias are unparalleled.

Byron is our local hipster and has the best mustache in the neighborhood according to a scientific study. He works hard sitting around in the cafe to give us our signature cool atmosphere.
